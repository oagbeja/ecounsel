Welcome to NOUN Automated Counselling System (ACS)

You are specially welcome to NOUN Automated Counselling System website. Relax and be free to explore the site 
for maximum result!

NOUN Automated Counselling System is a web-based counselling program for the general public, prospective 
students and staff who wants to know more about NOUN or wants to be counselled on what choice of programme to 
make. The programme is designed to help prospective students and staff access counselling services on-line. It 
also guide students on the University payment of fees procedures, course scheduling, course registration and 
other enrolment activities. 

Prospective students and staff are encouraged to visit the site frequently to avail themselves of the services
provided therein.
